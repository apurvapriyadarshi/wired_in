Finding Room

The team of PCCUBE went in Hotel Taj to attend a meeting. Meeting was in room number 37. When the team reached hotel there was no one to help them in finding room. As they were late for meeting they started searching room number 37. There are more than hundred rooms in the hotel.

Total number of rooms in hotel is not given. Your task is to stop taking input once room number 37 is found and print "ROOM FOUND".

Input

Keep on taking room number as input until room number 37 is found.

Output

Print "ROOM FOUND" once room number 37 is found.

Ex:

Input

1
2
4
10
37

Output

ROOM FOUND
